AZOG AUTOMOBILES COMPANY LIMITED.

Prestige Automobiles Company Limited (PAC) established in 2013 is to carry out the business of official importer and distributor of BMW Vehicles and Genuine BMW parts and to provide BMW services.

PAC delivers a full-fledged BMW showroom with a total building area of more than 6,000sqm. This facility comprises a display area that can hold up to eight BMW vehicles, a customer lounge area, and has more than 50 visitor parking lots located both indoors and outdoors with a Service Centre that can service up to more than 200 cars per month.

Our Company employed over 100 employees. We also have BMW-trained technicians equipped with advanced tools and a full inventory of genuine BMW parts and accessories to take care of these highly-advanced premium vehicles.
